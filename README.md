## About me

> Everything I build comes from the same place —  
> living with psychiatric disability since age 14,  
> and finding that most systems were built without us,  
> not designed for us,  
> and those who built them  
> were the ones to judge how well we fit.  
> That's why I build — with Go, with IoT, and with skin in the game,  
> to redesign systems for people like us.
